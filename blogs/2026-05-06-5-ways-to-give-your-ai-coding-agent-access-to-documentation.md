AI coding agents are popular among developers. Many of them are vibe coding and orchestrating multiple AI agents to accomplish their tasks rather than manually writing code. AI agents are good at performing tasks if they are provided with a good context. Providing access to the right documentation, such as API references, helps the AI coding agent build the right integrations. Without the right documentation, AI agents might hallucinate and produce code that may not work. This blog covers five effective ways to give your AI agents access to documentation.
